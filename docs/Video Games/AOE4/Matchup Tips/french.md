# French

## General Tips:

- French is a good late game civ, with cheaper units, good knights and strong trade.
- When aging up to castle, it is a good idea to send about 4 vills to stone to start getting that keep sometime soon.
- Early Guild Hall building allows you to either extend the pressure you have over the game by getting more food as food is running out in the map, or go for an imp play, even an imp keep drop.
- Red Palace in base from Guild Hall usually allows for a relatively safe farm transition

## vs Byzantine

### Gameplay Plan

Spear opening? Secret genius?

Skip pro scouts and apply heavy pressure, go to map resources early. Play Knights archers and try to find good fights. Do your best to force him to invest a lot of resources into defending his berries, this should allow you to go castle and apply more pressure.

Watch out for stables and add towers to defend vills on outside resources.

### General Tips

Archers do a good job of sniping Camel Riders.

## vs Chinese

### Gameplay Plan

Early wheelbarrow -> ps -> pressure him and try to outplay -> Castle at 12 minutes with Guild Hall -> 4-6 on stone for keep -> Relics -> try to deny the boar from him -> go for a kill on minute 18, use Guild Hall to push with more Knights, make siege.

### General Tips

You should have more units when you both run out of food thanks to Guild Hall and keep discount. That is especially true if you didn't lose vills and if he was unable to secure the boar.

Keep 2 knights there if you can spare them. After Barbican goes up, impossible to raid
