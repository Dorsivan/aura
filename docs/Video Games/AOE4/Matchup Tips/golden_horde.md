## vs Japanese

### Gameplay Plan

4 to food, 1 build ovoo + golden tent, rally 2 to gold.

Scout for his opening, if he makes spears (and he will), you finish gathering 200 gold, and then move to wood (keep 4 on gold) then you tower to defend the ovoo, and build a ger next to the tower, sending a sheep or 2

If they fast castle after that, you play agressive with Torguud, archers and spears and push him off of gold. Prioritize archers in this case, and move to spears when the enemy age up starts.

Deny resources and go to castle slower then you usually do, since you do have "knights" and your age up is slow

### Gameplay Plan

Spears, archers and Torguud, push his gold if he decides to castle up. Pressure him and deny gold, and go for castle with a nice mass of units.

If he plays feudal, add a few stockyards and go castle
